About us

Our story & mission

Founded over 25 years ago, Merigold School has been a cornerstone of quality education in our community.

Our mission

To provide every child with an enriching, inclusive, and future-ready education that builds character as much as academic skill.

Our vision

A community where every student leaves Merigold with the confidence, skills, and values to make a meaningful difference in the world.

Infrastructure

State-of-the-art classrooms, science labs, a well-stocked library, sports courts, a dedicated auditorium, and an art room.

Community

We foster a strong parent-teacher partnership and host regular events that bring our entire community together.

Proud milestones
